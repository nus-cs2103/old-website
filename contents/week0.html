About this document

  • This is your main source of information for CS2103/T. You will need to refer it weekly.
  • Recommended usage: At the start of each week, read the section corresponding to that week in the Schedule page. That should be good enough in general, unless you are keen to read ahead to find out what's coming up in future weeks.
  • Important bits of information are highlighted. Less important additional information are dimmed out.
  • We have given in this page the schedule for the entire semester, in case you would like to have a rough idea about what’s coming up in future weeks. However, note that finer details may be tweaked as we go.
  • It also contain a lot of links to additional resources. It’s ok if you don’t have time to read them now. They are for your future reference too (i.e. after the semester is over).
  • You can access this page after this semester using this link http://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/~cs2103/AY1516S1
