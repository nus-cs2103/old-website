Pre-Tutorial Learning Activities

T11A1. Reach V0.5rc

Similar to V0.4, but follow V0.5rc requirements instead. In particular,
  • Dogfood your product intensively and improve it based on your findings.
  • Bring code coverage near to your target.
  • After you have sufficient code coverage, fix remaining code quality problems and bring up the quality to the level you target.

T11A2. Relates XP/Scrum to your project

Pick task (a) or (b). Ensure at least one member picks each task and no more than one member picks the same concept/rule.

Preferably, try to pick concepts/rules that you think are not applicable to the project.

  1. Relate* three key concepts of Scrum to your project.
    e.g. product backlog, product owner, sprints, sprint backlog, daily scrum, user stories, scrumboard, story points, burndown charts, team velocity, scrum master, etc.
  2. Relate three XP rules to your project.

There is no need to bring any written answers; it is good enough if you can explain your answer verbally.

*“Relate” here means: Is it applicable in your project? If not, why not? Are you using something similar or an alternative in your project? etc.

T11A3. Relate reuse mechanisms to your project

Find platforms, frameworks, and libraries you are using in the project.
What are other alternative/additional platforms/frameworks/libraries you could have used in the project?

T11A4. Use Activity Diagrams and OO Domain Models

Use Activity Diagrams and/or Object Oriented Domain Models to explain the problem domain or the design of your project.
