Lecture 1: Starting from where we left off. Let's write a small program.

Overview

In this lecture, we start from where you left off in data structure modules. i.e., we assume you can write small programs without much trouble. Now, imagine you are putting those skills into use by writing a small program for your own individual use. e.g., A small program to store reminders-to-self. Let us look at some of the things that can help you do that. But before that, we take a look at our subject (part 1) and learn a bit about how the module is structured.
